Lions

Here are some fun facts about lions.

  • Lions weigh up to 250 kgs
  • lion's roar can be heard up to 8 kms
  • Female lions hunt, while male lions guard their teritory and their cubs

What to do when lions sneaks up to you.

  1. Freeze.
  2. Dont make eye contact.
  3. Wave your arms above your head and scream as loud as you can!
